Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Expressions India operates the Expressions India mobile application. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive the following information from your Google account:

  • Your name
  • Your email address

We do not collect passwords, payment information, location data, contacts, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your name and email solely to:

  • Create and identify your account within the App
  • Display your name in the App interface
  • Grant access to content you are enrolled in

We do not use your information for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens are stored on your device using the operating system's secure storage (Android Keystore). All communication between the App and our servers is encrypted in transit via HTTPS. We do not store your Google password — sign-in is handled entirely by Google's OAuth 2.0 system.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third party. Your data is used only within the Expressions India platform.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you would like your data deleted, contact us at the email below and we will remove your account and associated data within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
